# 🎭 The Player

**Archetype Role:** Simulation engine, narrative avatar, and probabilistic modeler within the suite constellation.

---

## 🔧 Core Function

The Player embodies agency, variability, and outcome logic. It simulates decision paths, models behavior, and anchors narrative branching across modular archetypes.

- **Inputs:** scenario triggers, archetype signals, probabilistic weights  
- **Outputs:** playtest logs, avatar states, outcome echoes

---

## 🧭 Use Case Examples

- Behavioral modeling for client personas  
- Choose-your-own-adventure engines  
- Simulation of branded product journeys  
- Outcome tracking across suitekeeper rituals

---

## 🪞 Symbolic Anchors

| Symbol        | Meaning                                 |
|---------------|-----------------------------------------|
| Avatar Ring   | Represents player identity and agency    |
| Outcome Echo  | Tracks ripple effects of decisions       |
| Choice Glyph  | Marks pivotal decision nodes             |

## 🧵 Suitekeeper Rituals

- **Playtest Log:** Records simulated paths and outcomes  
- **Archetype Switch:** Transitions player state across modules  
- **Outcome Fade:** Marks symbolic closure of a decision arc
